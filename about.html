About us:

Xin chào and welcome to My Journey – your go-to travel companion for exploring the soul of Vietnam.

We’re a team of passionate travelers, storytellers, and locals who believe that Vietnam is more than just a stop on the Southeast Asia backpacking trail.

Our journey began with a simple love for Vietnam. What started as a personal blog documenting weekend getaways and motorbike road trips has grown into a full-fledged travel guide created by people who live here, travel here, and truly know the ins and outs of this country.

We believe the best travel experiences come from local insight. That’s why we’re all about authentic recommendations, cultural context, and practical tips that help you travel smarter and more responsibly.

What you’ll find here:

  • In-depth travel guides to cities, towns, and off-the-beaten-path gems
  • Food explorations from street stalls to hidden cafes
  • Local tips on transport, costs, customs, and must-knows
  • Personal stories and reflections that bring destinations to life
  • Sustainable travel advice to help you leave a positive impact

    • At the heart of My Journey is a love for storytelling and a deep respect for the people, culture, and natural beauty that make this country so unique.
